# Data Dictionary

## cpi_food.csv

Columns:
- `date`: Date of observation (YYYY-MM-DD)
- `cpi_food_index`: Consumer Price Index for food products (base year 2010 = 100)
- `cpi_food_percent_change`: Monthly percent change in food CPI

Source: Rosstat (Russian Federal State Statistics Service)

## food_imports.csv

Columns:
- `date`: Date of observation (YYYY-MM-DD) 
- `import_value_usd`: Total value of food imports in USD
- `import_volume_tonnes`: Total volume of food imports in tonnes
- `import_share_food`: Share of food imports in total imports (percentage)

Source: Russian Customs Service and Federal Customs Service of Russia
